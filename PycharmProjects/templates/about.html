About Cook It!

**Cook It!** is a recipe search application designed to make cooking easy and enjoyable by helping you find recipes based on the ingredients you have on hand. No need for extra trips to the store!

With a focus on simplicity, Cook It! allows users to input ingredients and receive a list of matching recipes. Each recipe includes clear instructions, images, and guidance, making it perfect for both beginners and seasoned cooks.

Whether you're looking to use up leftovers, explore new dishes, or create something with limited ingredients, Cook It! is here to inspire creativity in the kitchen.

Happy cooking!
